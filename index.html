Places We Recommend Visiting

Trinity College & Book of Kells

Ireland's oldest university with the breathtaking Long Room library and famous 9th-century Book of Kells manuscript.

Tip: Visit early to avoid crowds.

Ha'penny Bridge

Dublin's iconic pedestrian bridge over the River Liffey, perfect for photos especially at sunset.

Temple Bar District

Vibrant cultural quarter with street art, galleries, and live music (daytime visits recommended).

National Gallery of Ireland

Free admission to view masterpieces from Irish and European artists across centuries.

National Museum of Ireland

Fascinating archaeology exhibits including Celtic gold and ancient bog bodies.

The Spire of Dublin

120m tall modern monument in the city center, a great meeting point.

Penneys & Dunne's Stores

Classic Irish retailers for affordable fashion and quality Irish-made goods.

Chester Beatty Library

Exceptional collection of Islamic manuscripts and world art (free admission).

Prayer Spaces in Dublin

Dublin City Mosque

163 South Circular Road, Dock 1

Convenient city center location with regular prayers and Jumu'ah.

Islamic Cultural Centre of Ireland

19 Roebuck Road, Clonskeagh

Large mosque with excellent facilities including wudu areas and library.

Faizan E Madinah Mosque

1 Leighton Court, Dublin 6

Beautiful mosque with welcoming atmosphere and separate prayer spaces.

Prayer Room at Dublin Airport

Terminal 1, near Gate 34

Multifaith prayer room available 24/7 for travelers.

Halal Dining Options

Bambino's Pizza

Authentic halal Italian pizza in Dublin city center (Desk 1 location).

Must-try: Their signature Diavola pizza.

Xian Chinese

Where we discovered Dublin's famous Spice Bag - a must-try halal Irish-Chinese fusion dish.

Marka Cadey

Authentic Somali cuisine featuring delicious sambusas, hilib ari (grilled goat), and canjeero.

Specialty: Try their Bariis iskukaris (Somali spiced rice with meat).

Keshk Cafe

Cozy Egyptian restaurant serving authentic halal Middle Eastern cuisine.

Zaytoon

Popular halal Persian kebabs and Middle Eastern dishes.

Umi Falafel

100% vegetarian but many vegan and halal options, famous for their falafel.

Dada

Halal Moroccan restaurant with beautiful decor and authentic tagines.
